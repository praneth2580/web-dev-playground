Getting Your AI Keys

Follow these steps to unlock magic features in Quote Writer.

1 OpenAI Key (Texts & Styling)

Needed for Magic Quotes and Auto-Styling.

  1. Go to platform.openai.com and sign up/log in.
  2. Click on Dashboard or your profile icon in the top right.
  3. Navigate to "API Keys" in the sidebar.
  4. Click "+ Create new secret key".
  5. Name it "Quote Writer" and copy the key starting with sk-....
  6. Paste it into the Quote Writer Settings.

2 HuggingFace Token (Images)

Needed for AI Backgrounds. (It's free!)

  1. Go to huggingface.co and create an account.
  2. Go to Settings > Access Tokens (or click here).
  3. Click "Create new token".
  4. Select "Read" access type.
  5. Name it "Quote App" and copy the token starting with hf_....
  6. Paste it into the Quote Writer Settings.
